Child Safety Standards

Last updated: 9 May 2025

This app is intended for users aged 18 and above. However, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of safety, including measures to prevent child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our Commitments

  • We do not allow or tolerate any form of CSAE content or behavior on our platform.
  • We use automated tools and manual moderation to detect and remove harmful content and accounts.
  • We require users to verify their age during registration.

Reporting Abuse

If you encounter any suspicious or abusive behavior, please report it immediately to our moderation team.

Contact us: web@sarc-iitb.org

Collaboration with Authorities

We cooperate fully with law enforcement and child protection agencies in all cases involving potential CSAE.

Thank you for helping us maintain a safe and respectful platform for everyone.
